Techsture shall strive to achieve optimum levels of information security by continue improving with the help of involving people, technology and appropriate environment/utilities in our course of operations to provide adequate confidence to our clients and interested parties regarding confidentiality, integrity and availability of information received, processed and transmitted by our company.

TECHSTURE SHALL:

  • Continually improve on information security controls and tools proactively using a systematic risk assessment and controls considering the threats and vulnerabilities on on-going basis.
  • Ensure to complete the projects on time without any security breach.
  • Comply with ISO 27001, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements as laid-down time-to-time national and international standards as applicable.
  • Ensure business continuity and minimize business damage by preventing and minimizing the impact of security events and continual improvement.
  • The organisation has an obligation to stakeholders, employees, customers and suppliers to protect the confidentiality, integrity and availability of information assets.
